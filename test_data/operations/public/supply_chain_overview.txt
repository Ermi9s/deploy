INTRODUCTION
------------
This document provides a high-level view of Apex Solutions' supply chain and
procurement process.  It is intended for all employees and approved partners.

-----------------------
  Category           Examples                         Owner
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  IT Hardware        Laptops, monitors, accessories   IT / Operations
  SaaS Software      Slack, GitHub, Jira, Notion      IT / Finance
  Office Supplies    Stationery, furniture            Operations
  Professional Svcs  Legal counsel, auditors, HR      Legal / Finance
  Cloud/Infra        Google Cloud, Elasticsearch      Engineering / Finance


PROCUREMENT POLICY SUMMARY
----------------------------
  Spend Level          Process Required
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  < USD 500            Manager approval via email
  USD 500 – 2 000      Finance portal request + manager approval
  USD 2 001 – 10 000   Two competitive quotes + Finance Manager approval
  > USD 10 000         RFP process + VP approval + Legal review

INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
--------------------
  • IT hardware inventory is managed in the Asset Register (Operations portal).
  • All assets valued > USD 500 are tagged and tracked.
  • Annual asset audit conducted in December.
  • End-of-life assets are disposed of via certified e-waste recycler
    (certificate filed by Operations for compliance).


DELIVERY & WAREHOUSING
-----------------------
  • Office supplies are delivered to HQ reception (Nairobi, Kenya).
  • Hardware orders are pre-approved by IT before delivery.
  • No personal deliveries to the office address.

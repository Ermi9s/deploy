APEX SOLUTIONS INC. — FACILITY SAFETY GUIDELINES
==================================================
Classification: PUBLIC
Department: Operations
Version: 4.0
Effective Date: 2025-01-01

---

PURPOSE
-------
These guidelines protect the health and safety of all employees, visitors,
and contractors at Apex Solutions facilities.  All personnel on-site must
read and follow these guidelines.


1. GENERAL RULES
-----------------
  • Always wear your visitor badge in visible areas.
  • Report any hazard or unsafe condition immediately to Facilities:
    facilities@apexsolutions.com or ext. 1001.
  • Do not prop open secure doors.
  • Only authorised personnel may access server rooms and utility areas.


2. FIRE SAFETY
--------------
  • Know the location of the nearest fire extinguisher, fire exit, and
    assembly point (marked on floor maps).
  • Do not block fire exits with furniture, equipment, or boxes.
  • In case of fire alarm:
    1. Stay calm and stop work.
    2. Use the nearest fire exit (not the elevator).
    3. Proceed to the designated assembly area.
    4. Do not re-enter until the all-clear signal is given.
  • Fire drills are conducted quarterly.  Participation is mandatory.


3. ELECTRICAL SAFETY
---------------------
  • Do not overload power sockets.
  • Report damaged cables or flickering lights immediately.
  • Unplug equipment at the end of each day where practical.
  • No personal heaters or high-wattage appliances without Facilities approval.


4. FIRST AID
------------
  • First-aid kits are located at reception and each floor break room.
  • Trained first-aiders: Laura Mensah (Ops), Fiona Tanaka (HR).
  • For medical emergencies: call the emergency line (112) first, then
    notify Facilities.


5. ERGONOMICS & WELLBEING
--------------------------
  • Adjust your chair so your feet rest flat on the floor and your screen
    is at eye level.
  • Take a 5-minute break every hour (the 20-20-20 rule for screen use).
  • Report ergonomic concerns to HR for a workstation assessment.


6. VISITORS & CONTRACTORS
--------------------------
  • All visitors must sign in at reception and be escorted at all times.
  • Contractors must present a valid risk assessment and method statement
    before commencing work.


7. CONTACT
----------

  Emergency Line     : 112 (national) / ext. 1001 (internal)

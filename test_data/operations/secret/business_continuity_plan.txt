1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
---------------------
This Business Continuity Plan (BCP) defines the procedures for maintaining
or rapidly restoring critical business functions following a disruptive event.
Scope: All Apex Solutions Inc. operations (HQ, remote employees, cloud services).

4. ACTIVATION PROCEDURE
------------------------
Step 1: Incident detected → Notify BCP Coordinator (Laura) within 15 minutes.
Step 2: BCP Coordinator declares incident level:
    • Level 1 (Minor): < 2 hour disruption. Local team handles.
    • Level 2 (Major): 2–24 hour disruption. BCP Team activated.
    • Level 3 (Critical): > 24 hours. Full BCP + Executive Notification.
Step 3: Activate communication tree (all-staff email + Slack #incidents).
Step 4: Initiate relevant recovery procedures (Sections 5–7).
Step 5: Log all actions in the Incident Log (stored in OKM Drive).


5. IT SYSTEMS RECOVERY
------------------------
5.1 GCP Outage
  • Failover: Apex does not currently have a secondary cloud provider.
    Engineers must activate static maintenance page and notify customers.
  • Target: Multi-region failover by Q4 2025 (Engineering roadmap).

5.2 Ransomware / Data Loss
  • Isolate affected systems immediately (network segmentation).
  • Do NOT pay the ransom without Legal and Board approval.
  • Restore from last known-good Elasticsearch snapshot (daily, MinIO).
  • PostgreSQL: restore from GCP automated daily snapshot (1-day lag).
  • Engage cyber insurer within 4 hours of confirmed breach.

5.3 Internet Outage
  • Primary: Safaricom Fibre (500 Mbps).
  • Backup: Airtel 4G router (pre-configured, stored in server room cabinet 3).
  • Backup activation time: 15 minutes.


6. REMOTE WORK ACTIVATION
--------------------------
In the event HQ becomes inaccessible:
  • All employees switch to remote work immediately (VPN + OKM systems
    are cloud-based and accessible from any location).
  • Team leads confirm remote readiness within 1 hour via Slack.
  • Client-facing meetings: inform clients within 2 hours; reschedule if
    video infrastructure is affected.

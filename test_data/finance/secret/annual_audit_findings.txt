AUDIT OPINION
-------------
Qualified Opinion — The financial statements of Apex Solutions Inc. for the
year ended 31 December 2024 present a true and fair view in all material
respects, except for the matter described in Finding A-001 below.


KEY AUDIT FINDINGS
------------------

A-001 — Revenue Recognition Timing Discrepancy (MATERIAL)
  Finding: USD 240 000 of professional services revenue recognised in
  December 2024 should have been deferred to Q1 2025 per IFRS 15 (performance
  obligation not yet met as of year-end).
  Impact: FY 2024 revenue overstated by USD 240 000; net profit overstated
  by USD 198 000 (net of tax).
  Action Required: Restate Q4 2024 accounts.  Implement a monthly revenue
  recognition checklist.  Due: 2025-03-31.
  Status: IN PROGRESS (restatement draft submitted 2025-02-20)

A-002 — Segregation of Duties — Payroll Processing (SIGNIFICANT)
  Finding: The same individual (Finance Analyst) both prepares payroll runs
  and approves them in the HRIS.  This violates segregation of duties.
  Risk: Potential for unauthorised salary adjustments.
  Action Required: Require Finance Manager second approval for all payroll
  runs > USD 10 000.  Due: 2025-04-30.
  Status: PLANNED

A-003 — Expense Claim Receipts Missing (MINOR)
  Finding: 12 % of expense claims (by value) lacked original receipts.
  Action Required: Enforce mandatory receipt upload in Finance portal.
  Status: COMPLETED (portal updated 2025-01-15)

A-004 — Inter-company Reconciliation Lag (MINOR)
  Finding: Inter-company balances between the holding company and Apex
  Solutions subsidiaries were not reconciled for 3 months (Sept–Nov 2024).
  Action Required: Monthly automated reconciliation report.  Due: 2025-05-31.
  Status: IN PROGRESS


MANAGEMENT RESPONSE
-------------------
George Mbeki (Head of Finance): "We accept all findings and have initiated
remediation plans.  A-001 restatement will be completed by end of March.
A-002 control will be implemented before the next payroll cycle."

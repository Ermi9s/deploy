APEX SOLUTIONS INC. — EXPENSE CLAIM POLICY
===========================================
Classification: PUBLIC
Department: Finance
Version: 3.0
Effective Date: 2025-01-01

---

PURPOSE
-------
This policy sets out the rules for reimbursing business expenses incurred by
employees on behalf of Apex Solutions Inc.


1. ELIGIBLE EXPENSES
--------------------
The following categories are reimbursable with valid receipts:

  Category              Limit (per occurrence)  Notes
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Business travel       No cap                  Economy class only < 6h
  Accommodation         USD 200 / night          Pre-approval > 3 nights
  Meals (business)      USD 60 / day             With client/colleague only
  Taxi / ride-share     USD 40 / trip            Airport transfers OK
  Conference/training   No cap                   Pre-approval required
  Team events           USD 50 / person          Manager approval required
  Office supplies       USD 50 / month           VAT receipt required


2. NON-REIMBURSABLE EXPENSES
------------------------------
  • Alcohol (except client entertainment pre-approved by Finance)
  • Personal clothing, gym memberships (not covered here — see benefits)
  • Traffic fines, parking violations
  • First-class or business-class travel without VP pre-approval
  • Expenses for family members not working at Apex


3. SUBMISSION PROCESS
----------------------
  1. Collect and retain original receipts (digital scans accepted).
  2. Submit via the Finance portal (finance.internal.apexsolutions.com)
     within 30 calendar days of incurring the expense.
  3. Claims exceeding USD 500 require manager approval before submission.
  4. Finance processes approved claims within 5 working days.


4. FOREIGN CURRENCY
-------------------
Expenses in foreign currencies are reimbursed at the bank exchange rate
on the date of the expense.  Attach your bank statement or card statement
showing the charge as evidence.


5. CONTACT
----------

WELCOME TO APEX SOLUTIONS INC.
--------------------------------
This handbook describes the policies, values, and expectations that govern
life at Apex Solutions.  Every employee should read this document in full
during their first week.


SECTION 1 — OUR VALUES
-----------------------
1. Integrity first: We do the right thing even when no one is watching.
2. Customer obsession: Every decision is tested against customer impact.
3. Continuous learning: We invest in personal and professional growth.
4. Collaboration over silos: Diverse teams build better products.
5. Accountability: We own our outcomes — both successes and failures.


SECTION 2 — EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
---------------------------------
2.1 Working Hours
  Standard office hours are 09:00–18:00, Monday–Friday (EAT).  Flexible
  start/end times (±2 hours) are available with manager approval.

2.2 Annual Leave
  • 21 days paid leave per calendar year (pro-rated for partial years).
  • Leave must be approved via the HR portal at least 5 working days
    in advance for requests ≤ 5 days; 15 days advance notice for longer.
  • Unused leave (max 5 days) can be carried forward to the next year.

2.3 Sick Leave
  Up to 10 paid sick days per year.  A medical certificate is required
  for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

2.4 Probationary Period
  New employees serve a 3-month probationary period.  A performance review
  is conducted at the 90-day mark.  Either party may terminate the contract
  with 2 weeks' notice during probation.


SECTION 3 — CODE OF CONDUCT
-----------------------------
All employees must:
  • Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity.
  • Protect confidential information per the NDA signed at onboarding.
  • Report unethical behaviour to HR or via the anonymous whistleblower
    hotline: ethics@apexsolutions.com.
  • Not engage in conflicts of interest without prior written disclosure.
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.


SECTION 4 — BENEFITS
---------------------
  • Medical insurance (employee + 2 dependents)
  • Pension contribution: Apex contributes 6 % of gross salary
  • Annual training budget: USD 1 500 per employee
  • Gym membership subsidy: USD 50/month
  • Home office allowance (remote employees): USD 100/month


SECTION 5 — GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
---------------------------------
Step 1: Informal discussion with direct manager.
Step 2: Formal written complaint to HR (diana.osei@apexsolutions.com).
Step 3: HR investigation (target resolution within 10 working days).
Step 4: Appeal to the People Committee if unsatisfied with outcome.

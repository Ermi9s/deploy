APEX SOLUTIONS INC. — REMOTE WORK POLICY
=========================================
Classification: PUBLIC
Department: Human Resources
Version: 2.0
Effective Date: 2025-02-01

---

POLICY STATEMENT
----------------
Apex Solutions supports flexible remote work arrangements that balance employee
wellbeing with team collaboration and business continuity needs.


1. ELIGIBILITY
--------------
Employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period and whose role
does not require a physical presence (e.g. facilities, security) may apply for
a remote or hybrid work arrangement.


2. HYBRID WORK MODEL
---------------------
Our default model is hybrid: employees work from the office at least 2 days
per week (Tuesday and Thursday are anchor days where all teams are present).

Full-remote arrangements require VP-level approval and are reviewed annually.


3. HOME OFFICE REQUIREMENTS
-----------------------------
Employees working remotely must ensure:
  • A dedicated, quiet workspace free from distractions during core hours.
  • Reliable internet connection (min. 25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload).
  • A webcam and headset for video meetings.
  • Company-issued device used for all work (personal devices require MDM
    enrolment approval from IT Security).


4. SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION
-------------------------------
  • Connect to the corporate VPN whenever accessing internal systems.
  • Never work from unsecured public Wi-Fi without the VPN active.
  • Lock your screen when stepping away.
  • Report lost or stolen devices to IT immediately: it@apexsolutions.com
  • Do not store confidential data on local drives; use the OKM Drive.


5. MEETING ETIQUETTE
---------------------
  • Join video calls on time with camera on (muted until speaking).
  • Use a virtual background if your environment is distracting.
  • Record important meetings for colleagues in different time zones.
  • Respect deep-work blocks (no-meeting windows: 09:00–11:00 daily).


6. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
-------------------------
Remote employees receive a monthly home-office allowance of USD 100,
auto-paid with payroll.  Additional equipment purchases over USD 200
require a pre-approval request via the HR portal.


7. CONTACT
----------
For questions about this policy: hr@apexsolutions.com

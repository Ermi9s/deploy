APEX SOLUTIONS INC. — CODE OF CONDUCT
=======================================
Classification: PUBLIC
Department: Legal & Compliance
Version: 5.0
Effective Date: 2025-01-01

---

OUR COMMITMENT
--------------
Apex Solutions Inc. is committed to operating with the highest standards of
ethics, integrity, and professionalism.  This Code of Conduct applies to all
employees, contractors, board members, and third-party partners.


1. ETHICAL BUSINESS CONDUCT
-----------------------------
1.1 Honesty and Transparency
  Employees must be truthful in all internal and external communications.
  Falsifying records, financial statements, or reports is grounds for
  immediate termination and potential legal action.

1.2 Conflicts of Interest
  A conflict of interest arises when personal interests could interfere with
  professional responsibilities.  Employees must disclose actual or potential
  conflicts in writing to their manager and Legal before engaging in any
  outside business activity.

1.3 Anti-Bribery and Corruption
  Apex Solutions has zero tolerance for bribery.  Employees must not offer,
  accept, or authorise anything of value to improperly influence a business
  decision, whether dealing with government officials or private parties.
  All gifts over USD 50 must be reported to Legal within 5 business days.


2. RESPECT IN THE WORKPLACE
-----------------------------
2.1 Equal Opportunity
  Apex is an equal opportunity employer.  Discrimination or harassment based
  on race, gender, age, religion, nationality, disability, or any protected
  characteristic is strictly prohibited.

2.2 Harassment and Bullying
  All forms of workplace harassment — verbal, physical, or digital — are
  prohibited.  Report incidents to HR (diana.osei@apexsolutions.com) or via
  the anonymous ethics hotline (ethics@apexsolutions.com).


3. PROTECTION OF COMPANY ASSETS
---------------------------------
3.1 Data and Intellectual Property
  All data, code, designs, and business plans produced during employment are
  the property of Apex Solutions.  Using company IP for personal gain is
  prohibited.

3.2 IT Resources
  Company systems and devices are for business use.  Limited personal use is
  acceptable if it does not interfere with work or violate security policies.

3.3 Confidential Information
  Do not disclose non-public information about clients, employees, financials,
  or technology to unauthorised parties.  This obligation continues after
  employment ends.


4. REPORTING VIOLATIONS
-------------------------
Employees who become aware of a potential violation should report it:
  • Directly to their manager or Legal
  • Via email: ethics@apexsolutions.com
  • Via anonymous whistleblower line: +1-800-APEX-ETH

Retaliation against anyone who reports a good-faith concern is itself a
violation of this Code and will result in disciplinary action.


5. DISCIPLINARY PROCESS
-------------------------
Violations may result in:
  • Verbal or written warning
  • Suspension without pay
  • Termination of employment
  • Legal action where laws have been violated

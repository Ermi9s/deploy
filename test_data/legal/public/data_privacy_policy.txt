INTRODUCTION
------------
This policy describes how Apex Solutions Inc. ("Apex", "we", "our") collects,
uses, stores, and protects personal data in accordance with applicable data
protection laws including the GDPR, CCPA, and Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.


1. DATA WE COLLECT
-------------------
  Personal Data:
    • Name, email address, phone number
    • Employment information (role, department, salary — for employees)
    • Usage data (login times, feature interactions — for platform users)
    • IP addresses and device identifiers

  Sensitive Personal Data (collected only with explicit consent):
    • Health information (sick leave records — employees only)
    • Financial information (expense claims, payroll)


2. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
------------------------
  • To provide and improve our services
  • To manage employment relationships and HR processes
  • To fulfil legal and regulatory obligations
  • To communicate service updates and security alerts
  • To analyse platform usage and improve user experience
  We do not sell personal data to third parties.


3. DATA RETENTION
-----------------
  Employment records : 7 years after end of employment
  Financial records  : 7 years (statutory requirement)
  Platform usage logs: 90 days (rolling)
  Chat/RAG session history: 12 months (user-deletable at any time)
  Security audit logs: 3 years


4. YOUR RIGHTS
--------------
Under applicable law, you may have the right to:
  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@apexsolutions.com
Response time: 30 days.


5. SECURITY MEASURES
---------------------
  • All data in transit is encrypted with TLS 1.3.
  • Data at rest is encrypted (AES-256) in all cloud storage services.
  • Access to personal data is role-based and logged.
  • Annual security audits are conducted by an independent third party.


6. DATA TRANSFERS
-----------------
  We process data in the EU, US, and Kenya.  Cross-border transfers are
  protected by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and adequacy decisions
  where applicable.
